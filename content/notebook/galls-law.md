# Gall’s Law

Siehe: [[Software Development]], [[Game Development]], [[Prototyping]]

>”*A complex [[Systeme|system]] that works is invariably found to have evolved from a simple system that worked. A complex system designed from scratch never works and cannot be patched up to make it work. You have to start over with a working simple system.*”
>— [[(John Gall, 1975) Systemantics - How Systems Really Work and How They Fail|John Gall (1975)]]
