[26.2] [Windows 96](https://windows96.bandcamp.com/); one of the pseudonyms of Brazilian vaporwave/electronic music producer Gabriel Eduardo. Sometimes I get a bit bored by the 80s retro aesthetic, but this is a really unique, fun/dancable, and kinda goofy (?) sound, with some extremely skillful production and synth work.
